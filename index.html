About Hello everyone. My name is Dilara and I am a dietician. In this site we have created, we are aiming for the better for everyone, thinking about how we can lead people to a more healthy life. It is a sad situation that we encounter with many wrong eating perceptions in our society, people getting used tofast foods more and more and making it a part of their lives. On this site, we have considered both encouraging people to live a healthy life, introducing them to new and suitable diets, and also considering healthier alternatives that anyone can make at their own home.
