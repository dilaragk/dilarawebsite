Avocado Toast

Ingredients

  • ½ avocado
  • 1 slice of white cheese
  • 1 whole wheat bread
  • 1 piece tomato
  • A little bit limon juice
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • 1 piece garlic
  • 1 egg

How To Make It

Take half an avocado on a plate and smash it. Add 1 slice of white cheese and mix it together. Add enough salt, pepper and lemon juice. Then we grate 1 piece of garlic and add it to our mixture. We put our mixture on our whole grain bread and add our cooked egg on top of it.

Oat Bowl

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp (tablespoon) oat
  • 1 tea cup milk
  • 1 whole wheat bread
  • Fresh strawberries
  • 1 tsp low in sugar hazelnut

How To Make It

First of all, we put our finely ground oats and milk and cook it on low heat. When it comes to a porridge consistency, you can take it on a plate and serve it with strawberries or fruits of your choice and at the same time low sugar hazelnut. If you don't want to use chocolate, you can add honey to your oats to add flavor. You can garnish with nuts or coconut powder as an extra.

Purslane with Rice

Ingredients

  • Half a kilo of purslane
  • 1 piece of middle onion
  • Half of tea cup rice
  • Half of tea cup oil
  • ½ tbsp of tomato paste
  • ½ tbsp of chili paste
  • 2.5 water glass of water
  • A teaspoon of paprika

How To Make It

After thoroughly washing the purslane, we chop it. We chop and fry the onions, then add paprika and tomato paste and chili paste and then continue frying. Then we add purslane and add our water. After boiling, we add our washed rice. After the rice is cooked, our meal is ready. Then you can serve it with regular yogurt or garlic yogurt if you want.
